Industrial Elevators

Heavy-duty goods lifts engineered to handle the toughest industrial demands.

Elevators

Built Sturdy, Built to Last

FUZI Industrial Elevators are specially designed for industrial purposes. These goods lifts are built sturdy and rugged to withstand demanding operational conditions without compromise. FUZI has designed goods lifts for weight capacities up to 5000 kg, and the size and capacity of each lift can be fully customised as per the client's specific requirements — ensuring a perfect fit for your facility.

Up to 5000 kg
Heavy-duty lifting capacity engineered for the most demanding industrial environments.
Rugged Construction
Heavy-gauge steel and industrial-grade components built for long, reliable operational life.
Custom Dimensions
Platform size and door opening fully tailored to your specific goods, machinery, and equipment.
Safety Systems
Overload protection, safety gear, pit buffers, and emergency stop at every landing level.
Easy Maintenance
Simple, robust mechanism designed to minimise downtime and keep your operations running.
Configurable Speed
Speed configurable based on load requirements and building height for optimal throughput.
