Hospital Elevators

Purpose-built for healthcare facilities — safe, hygienic, and always reliable.

Elevators

Engineered for Critical Care Environments

In healthcare settings, the reliability and safety of a hospital lift are paramount. FUZI Hospital Elevators are specially designed with wide doors, easy-to-clean surfaces, and smooth, quiet operation to ensure patient comfort and safety at all times. Built for quick and efficient transportation of patients, medical staff, equipment, and supplies between floors — with fail-safe systems for 24/7 critical operations.

Wide Door Openings
Extra-wide openings accommodate hospital beds, stretchers, wheelchairs, and medical trolleys.
Hygienic Surfaces
Anti-bacterial, easy-to-clean wall panels and flooring designed for clinical environments.
Silent Operation
Smooth, vibration-free ride minimises discomfort for patients being transported between floors.
Priority Call System
Emergency priority mode for ICU, OT, and critical care floors ensures fastest possible response.
Fail-Safe Design
Redundant safety systems and 24/7 backup power ensure operations never stop when it matters most.
ARD Power Backup
Auto Rescue Device ensures safe floor landing during any power failure — day or night.
