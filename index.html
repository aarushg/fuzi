Escalators

Moving People, Seamlessly

An escalator is a motorised moving staircase that transports people between floors with ease. Equipped with a co-moving handrail and emergency stop controls, our escalators combine reliability with safety for any high-traffic environment.

How It Works

Built for Continuous, Safe Movement

Escalators are powered by electric motors driving a continuous loop of steps along a pair of inclined tracks. A synchronized handrail runs alongside, keeping pace with the steps to give riders a steady grip throughout the journey.

Each unit is fitted with an emergency stop button for immediate halts and a key switch to restart travel in either direction — ensuring both passenger safety and operational flexibility.

Our Escalator Range

Three configurations engineered for different spatial layouts and traffic demands.

Type 01
Parallel Escalator
The most common configuration — multiple escalators mounted side-by-side running in opposite directions. Ideal for shopping malls, metro stations, and high-footfall commercial spaces that need continuous bidirectional flow.

Type 02
Crisscross Escalator
A space-efficient layout where alternating escalators cross each other at each landing, creating a staggered path. Perfect for buildings with limited floor area that still require multi-storey vertical circulation.

Type 03
Step Type Escalator
The classic flat-step escalator optimised for heavy-duty public transit environments — airports, railway stations, and large retail complexes. Engineered for durability, low maintenance, and long continuous operation.
