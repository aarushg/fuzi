Parallel Escalator

Continuous bidirectional flow — the most widely deployed escalator configuration.

Escalators

Maximum Throughput for High-Traffic Spaces

The Parallel Escalator is the most common and widely deployed escalator configuration worldwide. Multiple units are installed side by side, running in opposite directions simultaneously, providing seamless continuous up and down movement for large volumes of passengers. Ideal for shopping malls, metro stations, airports, commercial complexes, and any high-footfall environment that demands reliable, non-stop vertical circulation.

Bidirectional Flow
Simultaneous up and down travel maximises passenger throughput and eliminates bottlenecks.
High Capacity
Engineered to move thousands of passengers per hour efficiently and reliably.
Energy Efficient
Variable frequency drive reduces energy consumption automatically during low-traffic periods.
Safety Systems
Emergency stop buttons, handrail sensors, step-level monitoring, and anti-reversal devices.
Low Maintenance
Modular design enables quick part replacement, reducing maintenance time and operational downtime.
Flexible Angles
Available in 30° and 35° inclination to suit different floor-to-floor heights and layouts.
