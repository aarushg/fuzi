Capsule Elevators

A striking architectural statement — where design and engineering meet.

Elevators

Modern Design, Uncompromised Performance

FUZI Capsule Elevators are sleek, glass-enclosed lifts that serve as a visual centrepiece in any contemporary space. Ideal for modern homes, boutique offices, showrooms, and upmarket retail environments — they combine striking architectural beauty with full safety compliance and smooth, reliable performance. Despite their compact footprint, every capsule lift is equipped with all necessary safety features.

Panoramic Glass
Full or partial glass enclosure creates a dramatic visual focal point in any space.
Compact Footprint
Minimal shaft requirements make capsule lifts ideal for retrofitting into existing structures.
Designer Aesthetic
Curved profiles, LED ambient lighting, and premium materials for a truly luxury look.
Full Safety Suite
Safety gear, overload sensor, emergency intercom, ARD, and backup lighting included as standard.
Smooth VVVF Ride
VVVF drive ensures jerk-free starts and stops for a refined, comfortable experience.
Home & Commercial
Equally suited to luxury residences, boutiques, showrooms, and premium office lobbies.
