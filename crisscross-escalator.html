Crisscross Escalator

Space-efficient staggered escalation for multi-storey buildings with limited floor area.

Escalators

Smart Design for Space-Constrained Buildings

The Crisscross Escalator is a space-efficient layout where alternating escalators cross each other at each landing, creating a compact staggered path through the building. Perfect for buildings with limited floor area that still require smooth, reliable multi-storey vertical circulation without the larger footprint of a parallel configuration. An intelligent solution for modern urban buildings that need to do more with less space.

Space Efficient
Staggered layout uses significantly less floor area than side-by-side parallel configurations.
Cross-Flow Design
Alternating escalators at each level create a natural, guided passenger flow through the building.
Multi-Storey
Ideal for buildings with 3 or more floors needing continuous, reliable escalator access throughout.
Full Safety
Emergency stops, handrail monitoring, step-gap protection, and anti-reversal on every unit.
Energy Saving
VFD control automatically reduces power consumption during off-peak hours.
Durable Build
Heavy-duty truss and step chain engineered for years of continuous, trouble-free operation.
