Step Type Escalator

The classic heavy-duty escalator — built for the world's busiest transit environments.

Escalators

Proven Durability for Non-Stop Operation

The Step Type Escalator is the classic flat-step configuration optimised for heavy-duty public transit environments. Found in airports, railway stations, shopping complexes, metro systems, and large public buildings worldwide — it is engineered for exceptional structural durability, low maintenance overhead, and long continuous operation under the heaviest passenger loads, day after day.

Heavy-Duty Build
Reinforced truss and step construction rated for the highest passenger volumes and continuous use.
24/7 Operation
Built for round-the-clock use in the most demanding transit and commercial environments.
Easy Maintenance
Accessible modular components minimise service time and keep your escalator running with less downtime.
Safety First
Anti-reversal device, step-level sensors, comb-plate protection, and emergency stop as standard.
Energy Smart
VFD drive with automatic speed reduction during low-traffic periods to cut operating costs.
Multiple Widths
Available in 600 mm, 800 mm, and 1000 mm step widths to match your specific traffic requirements.
