Maintenance Services

365-day support, round-the-clock helplines, and expert AMC programmes — so your lifts never let you down.

Services

Keeping Every Lift Running Smooth & Safe

Our Annual Maintenance Contracts are designed to meet every customer's needs in a cost-effective manner. Our trained and appropriately skilled personnel regularly examine, lubricate, and clean the machine, motor, controller, bearings, and guides — making minor adjustments as required to ensure smooth and safe operation. Emergency breakdown services are also included in all AMC plans.

365-Day Breakdown Service
No matter the day or season, our teams are on standby to respond to any breakdown — year round.
24-Hour Helpline
Register complaints any time of day or night via our dedicated maintenance helpline — prompt acknowledgement guaranteed.
Emergency Response*
Priority emergency dispatch for accidents or passenger entrapment — our crews reach you as fast as possible, any hour.
Tailored Yearly Programmes
Specially designed annual maintenance schedules for every elevator type — keeping equipment in optimal, safe condition throughout the year.
Monthly Servicing
Regular scheduled visits by qualified and experienced technicians — following a structured maintenance programme every month.
Computerised Records
Every breakdown request is logged digitally, allowing us to identify recurring faults and take proactive preventive action before problems escalate.
Genuine Spares & Tools
A fully stocked materials store with authentic spare parts and the latest tools — enabling fast, first-visit resolution of most issues.
Mobile Technician Teams
Every technician is assigned a vehicle so they can reach your site quickly — minimising downtime with rapid on-site response.
Competitive AMC Rates
Most reasonable charges for maintenance contracts — transparent pricing with no hidden costs, tailored to your building's requirements.
Personal Management Attention
Senior management personally oversees the maintenance of every elevator we erect — because accountability matters at every level.

* Special Emergency service applies to accidents or persons trapped in elevator only.
